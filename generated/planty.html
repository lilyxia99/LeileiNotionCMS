This is a book that can be read at two speeds. It is both an animated flipbook and a comic. When flipped quickly, the plants on the left animate, but you can also read each line from the birds on the right page by page. This book is about the love between a bird and a potted plant: how to conclude a relationship where one side and the other have a "time difference" in their lives? First edition laser printed, handmade binding, limited to 50 copies. flipbook漫画，10cm*5cm*3.2cm(高), 2025
