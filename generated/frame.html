Frame

13’37''', 3840*1080p. Multi-media performance with hand-drawn animation projection, 2020

A documentarian, who always can’t capture the most important footage, tries to use documentary film to understand her grandfather’s death. Through this half documentary half fictional story, I reflected on how media is framing and limiting our memory. The video image I projected is shown below on the right.
