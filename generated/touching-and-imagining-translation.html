Translation & Online Seminar on Touching and Imagining: an Introduction to Tactile Art

workshop, 6/2021-2022, online.

With the permission of Jan Svankmajer, I initiated an online collaboration on translating his book Touching and imagining: An Introduction to Tactile Art. Based on our translation, we organized 3 online reading seminars to discuss and recreate the experiments in the book.

Apart from holding online meetings, we repeated the experiments in the book, shared our reading notes and documented all the discussion into documents.
