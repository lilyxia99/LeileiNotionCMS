Went To Learn Indonesian But Ended Up Learning Sign Language

Went To Learn Indonesian But Ended Up Learning Sign Language

Comic, 80pages, spiral binding with acetate pages inside, published with Icosa Magazine Sign Language Mail Pack.

In 2024 July, I went with Icosa Magazine on a linguistic trip to Indonesian. Together going was a deaf friend of us. The comic is about how I try to learn sign language along the trip, and all the bitter sweet friendship that evolve from it. There is curiosity, kindness, misunderstanding, complaints, and reunion.
